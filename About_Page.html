About Us

Closet Cat is a web app that helps you pick out your outfits. How does it work? Simple! You take pictures of your clothes and add them to your closet. Then you create an outfit by putting everything you want together! Create as many combinations as you want! You can also add them to your calendar! Go crazy with Closet Cat!
